Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about downloading X/Twitter videos.

Is it completely free to use?

Yes, X-Downloader is completely free. There are no fees, premium subscription tiers, or download limits. You can download as many videos and GIFs as you like.

Where are the downloaded videos saved?

Videos are usually saved in your browser's default "Downloads" folder. On smartphones, they will also appear in your Photos or Gallery app after downloading.

Why do I get an error when fetching a link?

This can happen if the tweet is from a private account (which cannot be fetched), the tweet has been deleted, the post does not contain any video or GIF, or if X is temporarily rate-limiting requests. Make sure the tweet is public and readable by anyone.

Does X-Downloader store copies of downloaded videos?

Absolutely not. The app acts as an on-the-fly streaming gateway to extract and deliver direct media files. We do not store, host, or cache any videos or metadata on our server.
